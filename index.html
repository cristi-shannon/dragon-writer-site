All About the DragonWriter™ App

DragonWriter is an app for SJSU Professional and Technical Writing students to track their progress toward a degree, minor, or certificate.

At the top of the Mountain rests your academic goal. All that stands in your way is a treacherous path of classes and a baby dragon who’s growing bigger by the day. Forge ahead toward the peak by completing courses and gain extra skills for your encounter with the Dragon.

Sync with Canvas

DragonWriter™ automatically syncs with your Canvas account to show real-time progress in each class you're enrolled in.

Compare your progress

View the leaderboard to see how you measure up to other students in the program.

Track your skills

Complete skills in and out of the classroom to collect jewels for your Spartan Shield.
